About Undercover Toast

Welcome to Undercover Toast, the board game where secrets are revealed and every slice of toast tells a story. Dive into a whimsical world of intrigue, strategy, and a touch of humor as you take on the role of a secret agent in pursuit of hidden truths.

Gameplay

Undercover Toast is a blend of strategy, bluffing, and wit. In each round, players navigate twists, decipher hidden clues, and outsmart their opponents. Whether you're a seasoned board gamer or just looking for a fun new challenge, Undercover Toast promises hours of excitement.

  • 2-6 Players
  • Approx. 60 minutes per game
  • Easy to learn, but challenging to master!
