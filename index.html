About the Game

Undercover Toast is a thrilling board game where strategy meets mystery. Players take on secret roles and navigate a game of hidden objectives and playful betrayal. Will you uncover the truth, or will you be toast?

How to Play

  1. Each player receives a secret role card.
  2. Navigate through rounds of challenges and debates.
  3. Collect clues and try to unmask the undercover traitors.
  4. The game ends when objectives are met or when the undercover players are revealed.

Strategy, negotiation, and a bit of luck are key to victory!
