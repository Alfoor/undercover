About the Game

Undercover Toast plunges you into a world of hidden identities and clever strategies. Assume a secret role, gather clues, and outsmart your opponents in a battle of wits. Uncover the truth before you become toast!

How to Play

  1. Each player is dealt a secret role card.
  2. Engage in rounds of challenges, debates, and secret maneuvers.
  3. Gather clues and deduce who is undercover.
  4. The game ends when objectives are met or the traitors are exposed.

Strategy, deception, and a pinch of luck make every game unique!
